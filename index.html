Hi, I'm Dan

I have always been driven by a deep curiosity for how things work. Early on, I spent hours exploring digital interfaces, using developer tools to deconstruct and understand their foundations — a curiosity that evolved during my fine art degree into building digital environments and applications.

Today I specialise in rapid prototyping and user-centred design in Figma, bringing complex systems to life with a focus on simplicity and intuitive experiences. My process is iterative: building, testing, and refining until every interaction feels right.
